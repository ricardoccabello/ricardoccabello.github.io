I Survey

How I document a retail survey

A useful survey begins before the measuring tape comes out. I first try to understand how the space is used, which routes matter, what furniture already exists and where the project is likely to become difficult.

I photograph the space in a sequence that another person can follow. Wide views explain the context. Closer images record junctions, services, finishes and anything that may affect fabrication or installation.

The drawings and photographs should support each other. Someone who has never visited the site should still be able to understand it well enough to make decisions.
