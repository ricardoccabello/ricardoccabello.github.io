III Installation

What I learned from overnight retail installations

An overnight installation makes every unclear decision visible.

The team has limited time, the department store still has rules to follow and the space must be ready before opening. Missing dimensions, undefined finishes or unclear responsibilities become immediate problems.

The best preparation is not more paperwork. It is the right information, organised so the people on site can act without waiting for an explanation.
