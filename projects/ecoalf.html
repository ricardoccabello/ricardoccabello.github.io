I Retail design and technical development

Ecoalf retail development

Five stores across Spain and Portugal, developed for permanent corners and temporary spaces inside El Corte Inglés, each adapted to a different footprint while keeping one brand system legible.

I

The challenge

Each location had different dimensions, circulation, existing furniture and installation conditions. The brand needed to remain recognisable without forcing the same solution onto every site, so the same fixture kit had to flex across corners, pop-ups and full stores.

II

The work

I prepared current location information, proposed layouts, dimensioned plans, furniture schedules, elevations and documentation for production and installation. Existing elements were reused where they still supported the new layout, which kept lead times and budgets predictable across five very different sites.

III

What the project taught me

A repeatable system is useful only when it remains flexible enough to respond to the real conditions of each space. Five sites, one kit of parts, five different plans; the constant was the discipline of the documentation, not the shape of any single room.
